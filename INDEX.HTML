Artist Statement

In the top three designs, I dedicated my work to bring together a blend of realism and surrealism influenced by the story, "Alice in Wonderland". The three slides are about the influence of time. Time can indeed take the edge off certain memory but cannot fully lessen the feelings that accompany remorse or guilt, which is the overarching theme of my work.
